The Planets

Our Solar System is made up of the Sun, eight planets, their moons, dwarf planets, asteroids, and comets. The Sun provides the energy that makes life possible on Earth, while the planets show a wide variety of climates, surfaces, and atmospheres. From Mercury’s scorching days to Neptune’s icy winds, each world has its own story.

Mercury - The Speedster

  • Closest planet to the Sun.
  • A "year" lasts only 88 Earth days.
  • No real atmosphere, just a cratered rocky surface.
  • Temperature swings from 430°C (day) to -180°C (night).
  • Mercury has 0 moons.

Venus - The Hottest Planet

  • Distance: ~108 million km from the Sun.
  • Travel Time: ~5 months with current tech.
  • Gravity: 0.9g (similar to Earth).
  • Thick CO₂ atmosphere traps heat (runaway greenhouse).
  • Surface temperature: ~465°C (hotter than Mercury!).
  • Venus has 0 moons.

Earth - Our Home

  • Distance: 150 million km from the Sun.
  • Travel Time: — (we live here).
  • Gravity: 1g.
  • The only known planet with life.
  • 70% covered in water, breathable atmosphere.
  • Earth has 1 moon.

Mars - The Red Planet

  • Distance: ~228 million km from the Sun.
  • Travel Time: ~7–9 months from Earth.
  • Gravity: 0.38g.
  • Dusty, cold desert with the largest volcano (Olympus Mons).
  • Has polar ice caps and signs of ancient water.
  • Mars has 2 moons.

Jupiter - The Giant

  • Distance: ~778 million km from the Sun.
  • Travel Time: ~13 months from Earth.
  • Gravity: 2.5g (strongest in the Solar System).
  • Gas giant with a Great Red Spot storm.
  • Has at least 95 moons, including Europa and Ganymede.

Saturn - The Ringed Beauty

  • Distance: ~1.4 billion km from the Sun.
  • Travel Time: ~3 years from Earth.
  • Gravity: 1.07g.
  • Famous for its icy rings.
  • At least 146 moons, including Titan (thick atmosphere).

Uranus - The Tilted Planet

  • Distance: ~2.9 billion km from the Sun.
  • Travel Time: ~8 years from Earth.
  • Gravity: 0.89g.
  • Rotates on its side (tilted 98°).
  • Has faint rings and 28 known moons.

Neptune - The Windy Planet

  • Distance: ~4.5 billion km from the Sun.
  • Travel Time: ~12 years from Earth.
  • Gravity: 1.14g.
  • Strongest winds in the Solar System (2,100 km/h).
  • Deep blue color due to methane in atmosphere.
  • Neptune has 16 moons.
